    About Us

    “We come to you, solve your legal complexities, and simplify lawyering!”

    We introduce your new partner in business: MRT LAW a corporate and commercial boutique law firm. We continuously challenge the constraints of conventional thinking and want to change the traditional way of lawyering. We seek to mitigate, manage and minimise risk in your business - we become your in-house lawyers and legal department and offer you the comfort enjoyed by large corporations by having your very own legal team you can rely on for your business.

    We handle all your corporate, commercial, legal transactions, structures, compliance and disputes. We offer our service with fast turn-around time, transparent fee structures and put your business interest first, at all times.

    We offer tailored advices and result-driven solutions unique to your business. A consultation with us, feels like business as usual. We understand that for business owners and executives, time is a rare commodity. That is why, at MRT LAW, we strive to make our client’s lives easier by offering practical and effective legal advices, guidance and tools in building their business by our simple philosophy.
